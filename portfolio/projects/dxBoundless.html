WaterfrontExpress! About CV

Concept Statement

WaterfrontExpress!is a golf cart-based service that carries people to destinations on
the waterfront while providing them with more information about any additional attractions
or eventsfrom the help of friendly and knowledgeable staff who specialize in customer
service.

The free service is centred upon a central station we call Station One, where users
of all ages and abilities can book a ride on one of the electric powered carts, take
shelter, get advice, and browse merchandise year-round.This way all patrons feel comfortable
entering and inquiring about questions or concerns that they may have.

This service caters to any patrons who either may be in a hurry and need a quick ride to
their destination, or by any uninformed customers who may need a little help or
direction getting to their final destination. It solves this by providing quick and
easy transportation and customer service inquiries along the Toronto Waterfront.

Summary of Design Concept

We have designed a golf cart service for expansive tourist spaces like the Toronto Waterfront.
In order to create a more accessible and navigable place for everyone.

The problem which we observed is that spaces like the Toronto Waterfront are big and have
different kinds of services such as restaurants, events spaces, and cultural activities. It
is not easy to navigate for people who are walking, let along people with mobility issues.
There are no clear accessibility supports in the space. Additionally, patrons who are from
out of town may not be as knowledgeable or have a keen sense of direction.

Our golf cart service has one main station near Ontario Square and Harbourfront Centre. Each
cart is able to carry four people. Our carts are electric and will be stationed next to the
station. People can get help in the station for more information and recommendations about
the area, and once they decide where to go, they have the choice to book a free cart to get
to their destination.
